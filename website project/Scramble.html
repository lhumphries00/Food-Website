Breakfast Scramle

Ingredients Needed

  • hand full of shredded cheese(cheddar works best)
  • two Eggs
  • chopped Green Onions
  • cup of rice
  • salt

Instructions

Begin soaking rice about 30 minutes before you want to start cooking your meal. Once that is done add 2/3 water 1/3 rice and bring the water to a boil then reduce the heat and put a lid on the rice. Once the water has been completely soaked you know your rice is done.

Now that the rice is cooking go ahead and crack your egg on a med-high heat skillet and cook it till it is the type of egg you enjoy, sometimes you may have to cook it longer to get rid of the runny egg yolk.

Put the two together and you have a healthy and tasty breakfast scramble for the low! Add a few pieces of cooked ham or sausage and you have a full meal! Enjoy!
