Breakfast Bento

Ingredients Needed

  • hand full of almonds
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices of bread
  • block of sharp cheddar
  • 1/2 cup of peanut butter
  • apple

Instructions

Hard boil your egg and click here if you need help boiling your egg. slice the apple into slices, put in peanut butter and stick apples into it(as seen in picture) Cube your cheddar and add your almonds and cheese into the box. lightly toast your bread and slice into triangles.
