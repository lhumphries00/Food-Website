Fruit Crunch

Ingredients Needed

Adjust ingredients to amount you want to make
  • kiwi
  • apple
  • strawberries
  • peaches
  • pear
  • oranges
  • sesame seeds
  • baguette
  • small mint leaves
  • Cottage cheese or cream cheese

Instructions

Slice the baguette into thin slices of bread. Slice all fruits into shapes seen in picture. spread choice of cheese over slices of bread, lay fruit on top, sprinkle sesame seeds and add a small mint leave. Enjoy!
