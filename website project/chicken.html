Irish Chicken Noodle

Ingredients Needed

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp of poultry seasoning
  • pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2-3 tbsp fresh parsley
  • hand full of egg noodles
  • 6 cups of chicken broth
  • two chicken breasts(bone in or boneless work)
  • 2 potatoes cubed
  • 2 carrots sliced

Instructions

Heat olive oil in a pot over medium high heat. Chop the potatoes and add them to the pot. Slice the carrots and add it to the pot. Cook until potatoes is softened, about 5 minutes. Stir in seasoning, broth and chicken. Bring to a boil, add noodles reduce heat to medium-high and cook until noodles are tender. Stir in parsley and remove bay leaf before serving. Enjoy!
